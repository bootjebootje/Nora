"I'm having a wonderful time! I'm not tired at all, and it must be
almost midnight," Nora said. Something struck her, and she laughed.
"Oh, will the magic wear off at midnight? Will I turn back into a
pumpkin when the clock strikes?"

Illissa smiled and reached out to tuck a stray wisp of blond hair
behind Nora's ear. "No, the magic doesn't wear off at midnight. It's
much more powerful than that. It comes from you. You wanted something,
and so it came to be."
